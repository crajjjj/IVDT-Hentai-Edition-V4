Set the expression values to the allowed maximum expressions/phoneme strength. Expression Changes will operate within set base expression limits. 
Expression/phoneme strength will vary depending on the intensity of scene. slow scenes will have lower expressions/phoneme strength while intense scenes tend to be higher.

blowjobs and ahegao will make use of "BigAah" phoneme

in  non ahegao scenarios, PC will either use a combination of "Oh" + "Ohq" phoneme or "Aah" Phoneme.
